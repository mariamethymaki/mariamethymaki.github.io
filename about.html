Education

I was born in Heraklion, Crete and since 1998 I live and work both in Athens and Crete. I finished my undergaraduate studies in the School of Architecture, N.T.U.A in 2006 and continued my studies in the Sculpture Art Department A.S.F.A. of the School of Fine Art of Athens, Greece.

During my Fine Arts studies, I have undertaken Scenography courses and I am also a member of the Performance Group.

Work Experience

Since 2006 I work as a freelancer. .My portfolio includes several Architectural and Art projects of both large and medium scale.
